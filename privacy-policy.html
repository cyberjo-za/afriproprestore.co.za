This website is operated in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPI Act). We aim to process personal information lawfully, fairly, transparently and securely.

How we use it

We use personal information only for the purpose for which it was provided, including responding to enquiries, providing services, and communicating with you about your request.

POPI Act and your information

We process personal information in accordance with the POPI Act and take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, disclosure or misuse.

We do not sell personal information and we do not use your personal information for unrelated purposes without your consent.

This website may use basic cookies to remember your cookie banner preference and to support normal site functionality. We do not use cookies for advertising or to track you across unrelated websites.

If you do not want cookies, you may disable them in your browser settings. Please note that some site features may not function as intended.

Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. You may also object to certain processing and lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa if you believe your rights have been violated.
