Our Story

Built to reduce the damage that foreclosure leaves behind

"Foreclosure is not just a legal process. It is a value destruction event." AfriProp Restore (Pty) Ltd was created on that single insight, to reduce that destruction for South African homeowners, and to build a better outcome for everyone connected to the property ecosystem.

Mission & Vision

Infrastructure, not a brokerage

AfriProp Restore is designed as structured national infrastructure. We are working toward a future where thousands of cases a year are handled with dignity, where insured clients feel protected rather than exposed, and where investors have transparent, verified access to genuine property opportunities.

We are not just a distressed property platform. We are a structured financial rehabilitation infrastructure designed to stabilise homes, preserve equity, and improve outcomes for every institution and family we work with.

Our Guiding Belief

"Restore your debts. Restore your home. Restore your life. Restore your dignity."

How Our Ecosystem Works

Every partner plays a defined role

AfriProp Restore connects regulators, banks, insurers, credit bureaus, contractors and homebuyers into one coordinated system built on transparency and fairness.

National Credit Regulator

Our processes are guided by NCR principles: regulatory oversight, credit standards and consumer protection.

Bank Partners

Providing home loans, mortgage financing and buyer funding that keep the recovery process moving.

Insurance Partner & Credit Bureaus

Offering risk protection, credit life and home cover, supported by credit reporting and insights from recognised bureaus.

SMME Contractors

Local plumbers, electricians, painters and builders carry out property rehabilitation, sharing fairly in the value they help create while growing local employment.

Legal & Conveyancing

Our legal partners handle the conveyancing and transfer of properties with full regulatory compliance.

Home Buyers & Investors

Families gain access to affordable homes and bonds, while investors gain access to structured, verified opportunities.

Together, we restore properties, rebuild value, and recreate opportunities for a better future.

Our Leadership

Meet the people behind AfriProp Restore

Thato Molefe

Chief Executive Officer & Founder

With over 20 years of diversified experience across the property, retail and banking sectors, Thato is a seasoned commercial leasing and investment property real estate broker.
