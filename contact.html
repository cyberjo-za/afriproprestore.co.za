Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions before you reach out

How does the process work if I am a distressed homeowner?

We start with an honest assessment of your situation, then route you to the most suitable combination of debt, refinance or rehabilitation support, coordinating the process on your behalf before matters escalate to foreclosure.

How can I get involved as an investor?

We offer subscription based access to a verified, structured pipeline of property opportunities. Contact us and we will send through our detailed investor information and current opportunities.

Is AfriProp Restore aligned with the National Credit Regulator?

Yes. Our processes are built around NCR principles, including regulatory oversight, credit standards and consumer protection.

Can my business partner with AfriProp Restore?

Yes. We work with banks, law firms, estate agents, suppliers and SMME contractors nationally. Reach out through this page and we will discuss the best fit for your business.
