What We Do

Structured services that stop distress before it becomes loss

AfriProp Restore acts as the coordination layer between distressed homeowners and the wider financial ecosystem. Every service below exists to protect equity, preserve dignity and keep South African families in their homes wherever possible.

Our Process

How a case moves through AfriProp Restore

1

Enquiry & Assessment

We assess the homeowner or property situation to understand the level of distress and what options exist.

2

Onboarding

Clients are onboarded with full transparency, so they understand every step ahead of them.

3

Structured Support

We route and coordinate the right combination of debt, refinance, legal and rehabilitation solutions.

4

Restoration

Properties are rehabilitated, credit positions are improved, and equity is protected wherever possible.

5

Ongoing Relationship

Clients, investors and partners stay connected through referrals, reporting and continued support.

Our Core Services

A complete rehabilitation toolkit

🛑

Early Intervention & Case Assessment

We identify distress signals early and assess each case before legal escalation becomes unavoidable, giving homeowners real options.

🧭

Structured Case Coordination

We route homeowners to the right combination of debt review, restructuring or refinancing partners, and coordinate the process from start to finish.

🏠

Property Rehabilitation Management

Our provincial network of plumbers, electricians, painters and builders restores distressed properties, transforming them from liability back to market value.

📊

Credit Rehabilitation Support

Working alongside recognised credit bureaus, we help clients understand and rebuild their credit standing over time.

🛡️

Embedded Insurance Solutions

Affordable credit life and home cover policies give clients peace of mind and protection while they rebuild.

🤝

Affordable Homeownership Facilitation

We connect qualifying buyers with bond finance and affordable housing stock, turning restored properties into new beginnings for South African families.

💼

Investor & Partner Access

Investors gain structured, subscription based access to a verified pipeline of property opportunities, supported by transparent reporting.

🏦

Professional Panel Partnerships

Banks, attorneys, estate agents and suppliers benefit from a coordinated referral network built for volume and reliability.

📍

National Provincial Coverage

Dedicated local teams operate province by province, creating jobs and empowering communities across the country.
